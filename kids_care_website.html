About Kids Care Inc.

We are a team of dedicated professionals committed to helping children with autism spectrum disorders reach their full potential through evidence-based interventions and compassionate care.

Our Mission

We are committed to ensuring all children on the autism spectrum receive the support they need, when they need it, meeting them where they are and helping them progress as quickly as possible. Our mission is to empower children with autism to better express themselves, engage meaningfully with the world, and enjoy life to the fullest.

Our Vision

We envision a world where children on the autism spectrum have the communication skills, academic abilities, and life skills necessary to increase their everyday independence. We imagine a future where every day on the autism spectrum begins and ends with smiles.

Our Services

We provide comprehensive, evidence-based interventions designed to help children with autism spectrum disorders develop essential skills and reach their full potential.

Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA) Therapy

Our ABA therapy program is the cornerstone of our services, utilizing evidence-based strategies to help children develop communication skills, social behaviors, and reduce challenging behaviors. Our certified Board Certified Behavior Analysts (BCBAs) and Registered Behavior Technicians (RBTs) work closely with each child to create individualized treatment plans.

  • One-on-one intensive therapy sessions
  • Data-driven treatment planning and progress monitoring
  • Focus on functional communication and social skills
  • Behavior reduction strategies for challenging behaviors

Early Intensive Developmental & Behavioral Intervention (EIDBI)

Our EIDBI program provides comprehensive early intervention services for children under 21 with autism spectrum disorders or related conditions. This Minnesota Health Care Programs benefit is designed to address the unique developmental needs of children with ASD.

  • Comprehensive developmental assessments
  • Individualized treatment planning
  • Family training and support services
  • Coordination with healthcare providers

Speech & Language Therapy

Comprehensive speech and language services to improve communication skills, including:

  • • Expressive and receptive language development
  • • Alternative communication methods (AAC)
  • • Social communication skills
  • • Articulation and phonological intervention

Occupational Therapy

Specialized OT services focusing on daily living skills and sensory integration:

  • • Fine and gross motor skill development
  • • Sensory processing interventions
  • • Self-care and independence skills
  • • School readiness preparation

Family Training & Support

Empowering families with the tools and strategies they need:

  • • Parent education and training sessions
  • • Home-based strategy implementation
  • • Sibling support programs
  • • Crisis intervention support

School Collaboration

Working with educational teams for seamless transitions:

  • • IEP/504 plan consultation
  • • Classroom observation and support
  • • Teacher training and education
  • • Transition planning and support

Insurance & Coverage

We accept most major insurance plans and work with families to ensure access to necessary services. Our team will help verify your benefits and navigate the authorization process.

Getting Started

Our comprehensive intake process ensures your child receives personalized care from day one. Here's what you can expect when you choose Kids Care Inc.

1

Initial Contact & Consultation

Begin your journey with a comprehensive phone consultation where we discuss your child's needs, current challenges, and goals. Our intake specialist will explain our services and help determine if our program is the right fit for your family.

  • Free 30-minute consultation call
  • Discussion of child's current needs and goals
  • Insurance verification and benefits explanation
2

Comprehensive Assessment

Our Board Certified Behavior Analyst (BCBA) conducts a thorough assessment of your child's skills, behaviors, and learning style. This comprehensive evaluation forms the foundation of your child's individualized treatment plan.

  • Direct observation and skill assessment
  • Parent/caregiver interviews and questionnaires
  • Review of previous assessments and reports
3

Individualized Treatment Plan

Based on the assessment results, our team develops a comprehensive, individualized treatment plan with specific, measurable goals. We present this plan to you and discuss implementation strategies and expectations.

  • SMART goals (Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound)
  • Detailed intervention strategies and techniques
  • Family training and home programming recommendations
4

Service Implementation

Your child begins receiving services with our team of qualified professionals. We maintain regular communication with your family and continuously monitor progress to ensure optimal outcomes.

  • Dedicated therapy team assignment
  • Regular progress monitoring and data collection
  • Ongoing family communication and support

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the assessment process take?

The comprehensive assessment typically takes 4-6 hours and may be completed over multiple sessions to ensure your child is comfortable and engaged.

What should I bring to the assessment?

Please bring any previous evaluations, medical records, IEP or 504 plans, and a list of current medications. We'll also provide intake forms to complete beforehand.

Do you provide services in our home?

Yes, we offer both center-based and home-based services depending on your child's needs and insurance coverage. We'll discuss the best setting during your consultation.

How often will my child receive services?

Service frequency is individualized based on your child's needs and insurance authorization. Typical programs range from 10-40 hours per week.
